* P1: $$T(1)=1, T(N)=T(N/3)+1$$
* P2: $$T(1)=1, T(N)=3T(N/3)+1$$

Then the correct conclusion about their time complexities is:  `[    ]`

A. they are both $$O(\log N)$$
B. $$O(\log N)$$ for P1, $$O(N)$$ for P2
C. they are both $$O(N)$$
D. $$O(\log N)$$ for P1, $$O(N\log N)$$ for P2
