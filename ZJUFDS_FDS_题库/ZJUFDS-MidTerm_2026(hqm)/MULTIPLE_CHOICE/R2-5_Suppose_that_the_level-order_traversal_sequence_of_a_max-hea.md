## R2-5 Suppose that the level-order traversal sequence of a max-heap is

A. 8, 17, 12, 26, 65, 82
B. 8, 26, 82, 65, 12, 17
C. 26, 65, 17, 82, 12, 8
D. 8, 17, 26, 65, 12, 82
